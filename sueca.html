Sueca

The card game that is so popular among Portuguese-speaking countries. Two teams of two players (allies sitting across from each other) divide a deck of cards among them and try to capture the most points for their team.

Team up with your ally: they'll give you more points of the suit you played or protect your play from the other team's attacks, using an ever-improving Artificial Intelligence developed from scratch by Epic Vortex, exclusively for Sueca.

Surprise your opponents with clearly marked gold trump cards that allow you to steal their points if you have no cards of the played suit.

Features include:

  • Rules page that teaches you how to play the game
  • Invalid plays are dimmed to help you identify the valid cards easily
  • Trumps for the current game show up as gold cards
  • Two different visual themes
  • Different options for many Sueca variants
  • Google Play achievements
